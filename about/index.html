Do you already do a ton of work, but always feel like you “should” be doing more? Or spend so much time doing for others that you struggle to get things done for yourself?

Is your team passionate and talented, but frustrated by competing -- even changing -- priorities? Does the phrase “lack of role clarity” haunt you?

Over the last decade, I’ve helped bring intention, strategy and joy to teams and organizations throughout NYC. And in that time, I’ve encountered countless talented, driven people who’ve lamented that if only they could be organized like me or motivated the way I was, their lives would be better.

I’ve also been a part of brilliant, hardworking teams who somehow struggled to get anything done beyond scheduling their next meeting, each person drowning under countless expectations and treading endlessly on the hamster wheel of burnout.

That’s where I come in.

It doesn’t take an all-day team retreat to get organized or the motivation of a tri-athlete to break these cycles. All it takes is a little time and a little intention. That’s all that’s standing between you and a saner, more satisfying life -- at work and at home.

Join me today, I’ll show you how to set the intentions that will change your life.

Define your work. Align your life. Design your future.
