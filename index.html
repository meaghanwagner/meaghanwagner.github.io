What is a Personal Strategist?

Across the business world, theres a cottage industry of strategists whose job it is to create plans, think them through, and deliver results. They are trained to create a vision and use everything at their disposal to make that vision a reality as efficiently as possible.

As a Personal Strategist, I work with you to apply this approach to your life, using all of the tools and tips Ive tried and loved (and a handful that Ive created) to help you let go of all that is holding you back, focus on whats most important to YOU, and get sh*t done.

 

Why do I need one?

Do you already do a ton of work, but always feel like you should be doing more? Or spend so much time doing for others that you struggle to get things done for yourself?

For anyone who has dreams but no time, is ambitious but overwhelmed, is an anchor for others but feels adrift themselves personal strategy is your path to taming the chaos that stands between you and your dreams. Even if that dream is just a really good nights sleep.

How does it work?

Personal strategy is fully customizable to your life and your needs, which is why I offer a free consultation so we can decide together the best plan to get you where you want to be.

Following our consultation, well check in on a weekly basis to identify your priorities and discuss how best to tackle whats most important to you.

And for an extra boost, I offer ad hoc tool and system building, to help kick start your biggest projects and go above and beyond for your most persistent struggles.
